Yan Margolin

Managing Partner

Yan Margolin is an attorney specializing in complex litigation, estate planning and business law. He has handled appeals, commercial litigation, complex fraud and regulatory issues. He is a member of the New York State Bar Association, and is admitted to practice in New York State and the Southern District of New York. He is a fluent Russian speaker who lives and works here in the metropolitan community.

Yan Margolin is a graduate of the University of Southern California Law School, ranked 18th nationally by U.S. News and World Report. He obtained his B.A. From New York University, where he was the recipient of the presitigious Baird Scholarship.
